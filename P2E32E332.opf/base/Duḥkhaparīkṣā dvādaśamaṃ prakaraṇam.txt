12duḥkhaparīkṣā dvādaśamaṃ prakaraṇam|svayaṃ kṛtaṃ parakṛtaṃ dvābhyāṃ kṛtamahetukam|duḥkhamityeka
icchanti tacca kāryaṃ na yujyate

svayaṃ kṛtaṃ yadi bhavetpratītya na tato bhavet|skandhānimānamī skandhāḥ saṃbhavanti pratītya
hi

yadyamībhya ime'nye syurebhyo vāmī pare yadi|bhavetparakṛtaṃ duḥkhaṃ parairebhiramī kṛtāḥ

svapudnalakṛtaṃ duḥkhaṃ yadi duḥkhaṃ punarvinā|svapudgalaḥ sa katamo yena duḥkhaṃ svayaṃ kṛtam

parapudgalajaṃ duḥkhaṃ yadi yasmai pradīyate|pareṇa kṛtvā tadduḥkhaṃ sa duḥkhena vinā kutaḥ

parapudgalajaṃ duḥkhaṃ yadi kaḥ parapudgalaḥ|vinā duḥkhena yaḥ kṛtvā parasmai prahiṇoti tat

svayaṃkṛtasyāprasiddherduḥkhaṃ parakṛtaṃ kutaḥ|paro hi duḥkhaṃ yatkuryāttattasya syātsvayaṃ
kṛtam

na tāvatsvakṛtaṃ duḥkhaṃ na hi tenaiva tatkṛtam|paro nātmakṛtaścetsyādduḥkhaṃ parakṛtaṃ katham

syādubhābhyāṃ kṛtaṃ duḥkhaṃ syādekaikakṛtaṃ yadi|parākārāsvayaṃkāraṃ duḥkhamahetukaṃ kutaḥ

na kevalaṃ hi duḥkhasya cāturvidhyaṃ na vidyate|bāhyānāmapi bhāvānāṃ cāturvidhyaṃ na vidyate
