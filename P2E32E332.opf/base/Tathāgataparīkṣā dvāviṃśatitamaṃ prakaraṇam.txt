22tathāgataparīkṣā dvāviṃśatitamaṃ prakaraṇam|skandhā na nānyaḥ skandhebhyo nāsmin skandhā na
teṣu saḥ|tathāgataḥ skandhavānna katamo'tra tathāgataḥ

buddhaḥ skandhānupādāya yadi nāsti svabhāvataḥ|svabhāvataśca yo nāsti kutaḥ sa parabhāvataḥ

pratītya parabhāvaṃ yaḥ so'nātmetyupapadyate|yaścānātmā sa ca kathaṃ bhaviṣyati tathāgataḥ

yadi nāsti svabhāvaśca parabhāvaḥ kathaṃ bhavet|svabhāvaparabhāvābhyāmṛte kaḥ sa tathāgataḥ

skandhān yadyanupādāya bhavetkaścittathāgataḥ|sa idānīmupādadyādupādāya tato bhavet

skandhāṃścāpyanupādāya nāsti kaścittathāgataḥ|yaśca nāstyanupādāya sa upādāsyate katham

na bhavatyanupādattamupādānaṃ ca kiṃcana|na cāsti nirupādānaḥ kathaṃcana tathāgataḥ

tattvānyatvena yo nāsti mṛgyamāṇaśca pañcadhā|upādānena sa kathaṃ prajñapyeta tathāgataḥ

yadapīdamupādānaṃ tatsvabhāvatvānna vidyate|svabhāvataśca yannāsti kutastatparabhāvataḥ

evaṃ śūnyamupādānamupādātā ca sarvaśaḥ|prajñapyate ca śūnyena kathaṃ śūnyastathāgataḥ

śūnyamiti na vaktavyamaśūnyamiti vā bhavet|ubhayaṃ nobhayaṃ ceti prajñaptyarthaṃ tu kathyate

śāśvatāśāśvatādyā kutaḥ śānte catuṣṭayam|antānantādi cāpyatra kuntaḥ śānte catuṣṭayam

yena grāho gṛhītastu ghano'stīti tathāgataḥ|nāstīti sa vikalpayannirvṛtasyāpi kalpayet

svabhāvataśca śūnye'smiṃścintā naivopapadyate|paraṃ nirodhādbhavati buddho na bhavatīti vā

prapañcayanti ye buddhaṃ prapañcātītamavyayam|te prapañcahatāḥ sarve na paśyanti tathāgatam

tathāgato yatsvabhāvastatsvabhāvamidaṃ jagat|tathāgato niḥsvabhāvo niḥsvabhāvamidaṃ jagat
