21saṃbhavavibhavaparīkṣā ekaviṃśatitamaṃ prakaraṇam|vinā vā saha vā nāsti vibhavaḥ saṃbhavena
vai|vinā vā saha vā nāsti saṃbhavo vibhavena vai

bhaviṣyati kathaṃ nāma vibhavaḥ saṃbhavaṃ vinā|vinaiva janma maraṇaṃ vibhavo nodbhavaṃ vinā

saṃbhavenaiva vibhavaḥ kathaṃ saha bhaviṣyati|na janmamaraṇaṃ caivaṃ tulyakālaṃ hi vidyate

bhaviṣyati kathaṃ nāma saṃbhavo vibhavaṃ vinā|anityatā hi bhāveṣu na kadācinna vidyate

saṃbhavo vibhavenaiva kathaṃ saha bhaviṣyati|na janmamaraṇaṃ caiva tulyakālaṃ hi vidyate

sahānyonyena vā siddhirvinānyonyena vā yayoḥ|na vidyate, tayoḥ siddhiḥ kathaṃ nu khalu vidyate

kṣayasya saṃbhavo nāsti nākṣayasyāpi saṃbhavaḥ|kṣayasya vibhavo nāsti vibhavo nākṣayasya ca

saṃbhavo vibhavaścaiva vinā bhāvaṃ na vidyate|saṃbhavaṃ vibhavaṃ caiva vinā bhāvo na vidyate

saṃbhavo vibhavaścaiva na śūnyasyopapadyate|saṃbhavo vibhavaścaiva nāśūnyasyopapadyate

saṃbhavo vibhavaścaiva naika ityupapadyate|saṃbhavo vibhavaścaiva na nānetyupapadyate

dṛśyate saṃbhavaścaiva vibhavaścaiva te bhavet|dṛśyate saṃbhavaścaiva mohādvibhava eva ca

na bhāvājjāyate bhāvo bhāvo'bhāvānna jāyate|nābhāvājjāyate'bhāvo'bhāvo bhāvānna jāyate

na svato jāyate bhāvaḥ parato naiva jāyate|na svataḥ parataścaiva jāyate, jāyate kutaḥ

bhāvamabhyupapannasya śāśvatocchedadarśanam|prasajyate sa bhāvo hi nityo'nityo'tha vā bhavet

bhāvamabhyupapannasya naivocchedo na śāśvatam|udayavyayasaṃtānaḥphalahetvorbhavaḥ sa hi

udayavyayasaṃtānaḥ phalahetvorbhavaḥ sa cet|vyayasyāpunarutpatterhetūcchedaḥ prasajyate

sadbhāvasya svabhāvena nāsadbhāvaśca yujyate|nirvāṇakāle cocchedaḥ praśamādbhavasaṃtateḥ

carame na niruddhe ca prathamo yujyate bhavaḥ|carame nāniruddhe ca prathamo yujyate bhavaḥ

nirudhyamāne carame prathamo yadi jāyate|nirudhyamāna ekaḥ syājjāyamāno'paro bhavet

na cennirudhyamānaśca jāyamānaśca yujyate|sārdhaṃ ca mriyate yeṣu teṣu skandheṣu jāyate

evaṃ triṣvapi kāleṣu na yuktā bhavasaṃtatiḥ|triṣu kāleṣu yā nāsti sā kathaṃ bhavasaṃtatiḥ
