17karmaphalaparīkṣā saptadaśamaṃ prakaraṇam|ātmasaṃyamakaṃ cetaḥ parānugrāhakaṃ ca yat|maitraṃ
sa dharmastadbījaṃ phalasya pretya ceha ca

cetanā cetayitvā ca karmoktaṃ paramarṣiṇā|tasyānekavidho bhedaḥ karmaṇaḥ parikīrtitaḥ

tatra yaccetanetyuktaṃ karma tanmānasaṃ smṛtam|cetayitvā ca yattūktaṃ tattu kāyikavācikam

vāgviṣpando'viratayo yāścāvijñaptisaṃjñitāḥ|avijñaptaya evānyāḥ smṛtā viratayastathā

paribhogānvayaṃ puṇyamapuṇyaṃ ca tathāvidham|cetanā ceti saptaite dharmāḥ karmāñjanāḥ smṛtāḥ

tiṣṭhatyā pākakālāccetkarma tannityatāmiyāt|niruddhaṃ cennirūddhaṃ satkiṃ phalaṃ janayiṣyati

yo'ṅkuraprabhṛtirbījātsaṃtāno'bhipravartate|tataḥ phalamṛte bījātsa ca nābhipravartate

bījācca yasmātsaṃtānaḥ saṃtānācca phalodbhavaḥ|bījapūrvaṃ phalaṃ tasmānnocchinnaṃ nāpi śāśvatam

yastasmāccittasaṃtānaścetaso'bhipravartate|tataḥ phalamṛte cittātsa ca nābhipravartate

cittācca yasmātsaṃtānaḥ saṃtānācca phalodbhavaḥ|karmapūrvaṃ phalaṃ tasmānnocchinnaṃ nāpi śāśvatam

dharmasya sādhanopāyāḥ śuklāḥ karmapathā daśa|phalaṃ kāmaguṇāḥ pañca dharmasya pretya ceha ca

bahavaśca mahāntaśca doṣāḥ syurapi kalpanā|yadyeṣā tena naivaiṣā kalpanātropapadyate

imāṃ punaḥ pravakṣyāmi kalpanāṃ yātra yojyate|buddhaiḥ pratyekabuddhaiśca śrāvakaiścānuvarṇitām

patraṃ yathāvipraṇāśastatharṇamiva karma ca|caturvidho dhātutaḥ sa prakṛtyāvyākṛtaśca saḥ

prahāṇato na praheyo bhāvanāheya eva vā|tasmādavipraṇāśena jāyate karmaṇāṃ phalam

prahāṇataḥ praheyaḥ syātkarmaṇaḥ saṃkrameṇa vā|yadi doṣāḥ prasajyeraṃstatra karmavadhādayaḥ

sarveṣāṃ viṣabhāgānāṃ sabhāgānāṃ ca karmaṇām|pratisaṃdhau sadhātūnāmeka utpadyate tu saḥ

karmaṇaḥ karmaṇo dṛṣṭe dharma utpadyate tu saḥ|dviprakārasya sarvasya vipakke'pi ca tiṣṭhati

phalavyatikramādvā sa maraṇādvā nirudhyate|anāsravaṃ sāsravaṃ ca vibhāgaṃ tatra lakṣayet

śūnyatā ca na cocchedaḥ saṃsāraśca na śāśvatam|karmaṇo'vipraṇāśaśca dharmo buddhena deśitaḥ

karma notpadyate kasmāt niḥsvabhāvaṃ yatastataḥ|yasmācca tadanutpannaṃ na tasmādvipraṇaśyati

karma svabhāvataścetsyācchāśvataṃ syādasaṃśayam|akṛtaṃ ca bhavetkarma kriyate na hi śāśvatam

akṛtābhyāgamabhayaṃ syātkarmākṛtakaṃ yadi|abrahmacaryavāsaśca doṣastatra prasajyate

vyavahārā virudhyante sarva eva na saṃśayaḥ|puṇyapāpakṛtornaiva pravibhāgaśca yujyate

tadvipakvavipākaṃ ca punareva vipakṣyati|karma vyavasthitaṃ yasmāttasmātsvābhāvikaṃ yadi

karma kleśātmakaṃ cedaṃ te ca kleśā na tattvataḥ|na cette tattvataḥ kleśāḥ karma syāttattvataḥ
katham

karma kleśāśca dehānāṃ pratyayāḥ samudāhṛtāḥ|karma kleśāśca te śūnyā yadi deheṣu kā kathā

avidyānivṛto jantustṛṣṇāsaṃyojanaśca saḥ|sa bhoktā sa ca na karturanyo na ca sa eva saḥ

na pratyayasamutpannaṃ nāpratyayasamutthitam|asti yasmādidaṃ karma tasmātkartāpi nāstyataḥ

karma cennāsti kartā ca kutaḥ syātkarmajaṃ phalam|asatyatha phale bhoktā kuta eva bhaviṣyati

yathā nirmitakaṃ śāstā nirmimītarddhisaṃpadā|nirmito nirmimītānyaṃ sa ca nirmitakaḥ punaḥ

tathā nirmitakākāraḥ kartā karma ca tatkṛtam|tadyathā nirmitenānyo nirmito nirmitastathā

kleśāḥ karmāṇi dehāśca kartāraśca phalāni ca|gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ
